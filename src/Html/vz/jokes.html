"How much does the average American worker earn in a week?"

Bibi asked, "And what does he do with the remainder?"

Clinton replied, "It's a free country, we don't ask."

"How much does the average Israeli worker earn in a week?"

Bibi answered, "About $1500"

Clinton asked, "And what are his expenses for a week?"

Clinton exclaimed, "That's impossible! How does he manage to do it?"

Bibi answered, "It's a free country, we don't ask."
